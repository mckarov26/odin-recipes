Pork Adobo Ingredients and Procedures

Pork Adobo Ingredients

  • Boneless Pork Belly
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Garlics
  • Onions
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Dried Bay Leaves
  • Soy Souce
  • Vinegar
  • Water

Pork Adobo Procedures

  1. Slice the Boneless Pork Belly into proper cubes
  2. Preheat your Oil on a pot, Note: Set to High Heat Settings
  3. Diced your Garlic and Onions
  4. Place your slice pork cubes into the pot and saute
  5. Place your Diced Garlic and Onions into the Pot, Saute it and mix them well with the Sauted Pork Cubes
  6. Pour a bit of Soy Souce and Vinegar into the pot mixed it well, follow the 2:1 proportions and wait for it to boil
  7. Put a half glass of water and wait for it to boil again
  8. Once the liquid sauce decreased, adjust the setting to low heat settings
  9. this time, taste it, adjust the flavour by putting the soy souce or vinegar

    10. Enjoy My #1 Favorite Food
