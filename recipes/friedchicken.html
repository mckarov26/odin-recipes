Fried Chicken Ingredients and Procedures

Fried Chicken Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Ground Garlic
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Ground Onion
  • Eggs

Fried Chicken Procedures

  1. Slice the Whole Chicken into proper portions
  2. Preheat your Oil on a deeper-fryer, Note: Don't put to much fire or heat under the fryer
  3. In a large bowl, combine the flour,salt,ground garlic,ground black-pepper and ground onion until thoroughly mixed
  4. Open the eggs and mixed them well
  5. Mix the Sliced Parts of Chicken to the mixed eggs thoroughly
  6. Dust it with a flour and mix it with the flour that has the rest of the ingredients in it
  7. After the mixed, put it slowly to deep-fryer
  8. Wait for it to become golden-brown
