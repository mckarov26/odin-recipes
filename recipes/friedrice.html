Fried Rice Ingredients and Procedures

Fried Rice Ingredients

  • Newly Cooked or Used White Rice
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Onion
  • Diced Mixed Vegetable
  • Garlic
  • Ground Black-Pepper
  • Salt
  • Eggs

Fried Rice Procedures

  1. Preheat your Oil
  2. Chop your Onions and Garlics and put it to the pan and Saute it with high heat settings.
  3. Open the Eggs and Mixed them well
  4. Put it to the pan then mixed the well with the sauted ingredients
  5. Put the rice and mixed them thoroughly
  6. Put the diced mixed vegetable and mixed it well
  7. Adjust the setting to low heat settings wait for 5min to 10mins for it too cook

    8. Enjoy your Food!
